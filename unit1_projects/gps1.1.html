Do's

  • Be courteous
  • Check in with your pair
  • Decide on Driver/Navigator Roles
  • Ask your pair to switch roles so you know your responsibilities
  • Keep communication open
  • Ask for opinions
  • Allow mistakes
  • submit feedback
  • use feedback

  • Be a jerk
  • Not respond to messages
  • Be late
  • Be disrespectful
  • go silent
  • play online games while pairing
